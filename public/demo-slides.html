The Problem

and locked inside closed networks.

Fragmented information
Hiring signals, market trends, interview prep, and industry news are scattered across LinkedIn, Reddit, newsletters, and firm websites — no single home.

Closed networks

First-generation finance professionals are most disadvantaged.
