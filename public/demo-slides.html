Introducing

Onlu

Free finance intelligence for students and early-career professionals
onluintel.com
The Problem

Breaking into finance is hard.
The information you need is locked away.

💸
Expensive
Bloomberg terminal costs $25k/year. Most research is paywalled. Students can't access the same tools as professionals.
🔍
Fragmented
Hiring signals, market trends, and interview prep are scattered across LinkedIn, Reddit, newsletters, and firm websites.
🚪
Closed Networks
The best intel travels through alumni networks and internal firm chats — inaccessible to most early-career candidates.
The Solution

One free platform. Everything you need.

💼
Hiring Watch
Real-time hiring signals from PE, IB, and hedge funds. Know which firms are building teams before jobs are posted.
📊
Market Pulse
Weekly deal flow, fund performance, and macro signals — curated for finance professionals, not casual investors.
🎯
Edge Prep
Interview prep for buyside roles. Concept Q&A, case library, and resume review — built by someone who's done the interviews.
📚
Onlu Learning + Table
Deep-dive guides on private credit, ABF, commodities, and more — plus small-group coffee chats for finance professionals.
